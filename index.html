About

Minimal build, clear thinking

I am a class 10 student from Kerala who builds projects with electronics, programming, and visual media. I focus on practical systems, intentional design, and work that is easy to understand.

This portfolio highlights embedded systems, firmware workflows, interface design, creative media, and research-led projects. Each section is structured to show the project goal, key tools, and how I approach a solution.

Electronics & Embedded Systems

Embedded Systems & IoT

I've recently been enjoying experimenting with microcontrollers and finding ways to connect software with the physical world. Most of these projects involve ESP32s, Arduinos, sensors, and wireless communication. They range from simple beginner projects to a bit more complex that taught me about how to use ESP32 and basics of embedded programming.

Robotics & Control Systems

Many of my projects involve controlling movement, including vehicles, servos, or any other mechanical system. I've been learning more and improving on using motor drivers to drive different types of motors, mapping rc controls to servos and wireless electronics. I'm still learning, and many of these projects continue to evolve as I improve them.

Audio Electronics

Audio is an area that I've become more interested in recently. While I haven't done many projects in this field yet, it's an area I hope to explore much further in the future. Building a custom Bluetooth speaker introduced me to amplifiers, speaker design, enclosure construction to produce high-quality sound, and the challenges of constructing a good speaker.
