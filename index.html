Connecting Ideas to Projects

I am a Class 10 student from Kerala who enjoys building projects with electronics, programming, and visual media. I like turning my ideas into real projects and learning through the process of creating them. Over the years, I have worked on electronics projects using microcontrollers like the ESP-32, audio projects such as a DIY speaker, and some basic wireless communication projects. I also enjoy art, design, and programming, along with hobbies like playing guitar, photography, and reading. This website is where I document my work, share what I learn, and showcase the projects and skills I continue to develop.

This website is part of ARCHA MEDIA, my personal brand and creative portfolio. I created ARCHA MEDIA as a place to bring together everything I build, design, and create while also laying the foundation for future creative and technical work. It showcases my projects, reflects my approach to problem-solving, and demonstrates my skills in areas such as web development, programming, electronics, and design. As I continue learning and taking on larger projects, ARCHA MEDIA will grow alongside me as both a portfolio and the identity behind my work.

Electronics & Embedded Systems

Embedded Systems & IoT

I've recently been enjoying experimenting with microcontrollers and finding ways to connect software with the physical world. Most of these projects involve ESP32s, Arduinos, sensors, and wireless communication. They range from simple beginner projects to a bit more complex that taught me about how to use ESP32 and basics of embedded programming.

Robotics & Control Systems

Many of my projects involve controlling movement, including vehicles, servos, or any other mechanical system. I've been learning more and improving on using motor drivers to drive different types of motors, mapping rc controls to servos and wireless electronics. I'm still learning, and many of these projects continue to evolve as I improve them.

Audio Electronics

Audio is an area that I've become more interested in recently. While I haven't done many projects in this field yet, it's an area I hope to explore much further in the future. Building a custom Bluetooth speaker introduced me to amplifiers, speaker design, enclosure construction to produce high-quality sound, and the challenges of constructing a good speaker.
